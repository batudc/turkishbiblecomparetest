About Us

Who
We Are

Holy Bible Study is an independent research platform built to make Turkish Bible translation comparison and the original Hebrew and Greek texts accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Understanding
Scripture
Deeply

Holy Bible Study is a digital research platform that lets readers explore differences between Turkish Bible translations and access original Hebrew and Greek texts.

We bring together centuries of translation heritage — from the Ottoman era to the present day — so every reader can study at their own pace.

Our platform is designed for scholars, theology students, clergy and curious individuals. All tools are completely free.

Our Core
Values

01

Accuracy

All translation data is drawn from primary sources. No text is altered or taken out of context.

02

Accessibility

The platform is entirely free. You can explore the original languages without any knowledge of Hebrew or Greek.

03

Neutrality

No translation is ranked above another. Each text is presented in its own context and linguistic framework.
